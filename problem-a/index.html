Remy

(the little chef from the movie, Ratatouille)

Remy is a character I admire very much. He is from the animated movie, Ratatouille, directed by Brad Bird in 2007. I admire his strength and determination, as he follows his dreams in becoming a chef. His reslience in the face of challenges encourages the audience to pursue one's passion(s).

3 Reasons I Admire Remy:

  • The blue-grey color of his fur
  • His sense of smell that can detect ingredients in food
  • His ability to read and understand English
